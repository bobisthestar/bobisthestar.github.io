Contests

Students can submit their work for the following contests:

Check out the Young Authors Writing Competition. Submissions open on November 17, 2020, and will remain open until January 31, 2021. The contest is open to students attending grades 9-12.

Also sign up for the Scholastic Writing Awards. Entry fees are $7 per individual entry and $25 per portfolio. If the entry fee is a barrier to your participation in the Awards, we will waive the fee when you submit a Fee Waiver Form. Entries for the 2021 Scholastic Awards open on September 1, 2020. The due date is December 4, 2020 11:59:59 PM.
